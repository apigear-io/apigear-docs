History​

ApiGear was born out of the need to integrate diverse software systems in a more efficient way. The first version of ApiGear was released in 2019.

The company behind ApiGear, was acquired by Epic Games Inc. in 2021 from ApiGear UG based in Germany. The founders of ApiGear UG where Jürgen Ryannel and Wolfgang Bremer. They are still working on ApiGear as part of Epic Games Inc.
